Since 1884, we have been revolutionizing the art of pickle making. Thanks to our unmatched experience and refined process, we lead the pickle market.

With over 20 years of experience in the pickle industry, our expert pickle cutters hand-cut every pickle.

Only the best pickles. All our pickles are fully inspected and tested at least 40 times before being packaged.

We package our pickles into premium glass jars, made of recycled glass. The jar is then pressurized to provide optimum conditions for the pickles.

Our water is special, too! We extract our water from a well deep under the Rokko Mountains in Kobe Japan to provide you with the optimal pickle experience.
